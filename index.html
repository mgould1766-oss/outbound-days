Outbound Journey

Day-by-day across Route 66

Day 1
Goldsboro → Lewisburg
The adventure begins. First stretch toward the mountains and your first overnight.
Day 2
Lewisburg → Tipp City
Rolling north through Appalachia toward the Midwest.
Day 3
Tipp City → Chicago Area
Positioning day. Tomorrow, Route 66 officially begins.
Day 4
Chicago → Springfield
Start of Route 66. Lou Mitchell’s, Gemini Giant, Cozy Dog.
Day 5
Springfield → Rolla
Chain of Rocks Bridge and classic roadside stops.
Day 6
Rolla → Tulsa
Blue Whale of Catoosa and Oklahoma fun.
Day 7
Tulsa → Amarillo
Wide open spaces. Cadillac Ranch and MidPoint Café.
Day 8
Amarillo → Albuquerque
Desert colors and New Mexico landscapes.
Day 9
Albuquerque → Holbrook
Standing on the Corner in Winslow and Wigwam Motel.
Day 10
Holbrook → Williams
Gateway to the Grand Canyon.
Day 11
Williams → Needles
Desert drive and Route 66 nostalgia.
Day 12
Needles → Buena Park
Santa Monica Pier. End of Route 66. You made it.
